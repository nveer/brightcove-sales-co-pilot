Overview

What This Plugin Does

GOOSE plugs into Claude Desktop and gives you a set of commands that automate the most time-consuming parts of your sales workflow.

🌅
Daily Call Prep
Say "prep me for my calls today" and Claude pulls your full day together — Google Calendar, Gmail, Granola meeting notes, Gong call transcripts, and Salesforce account data via Brightcove Gateway — then outputs a visual HTML briefing page. Any open customer question is auto-researched on support.brightcove.com and embedded with a ✅ before your first call.
Morning Ritual
📞
Live Call Companion
Monitors your Granola transcript in real time during a call. When it detects a trigger phrase, it deploys parallel agents to research answers from Brightcove docs and your account history — all while you keep talking. After the call: a follow-up subpage is created under the customer's Active Customers row in Notion + one copy-paste follow-up email, already written.
Real-Time
📬
Email Triage
Reads full email threads before drafting — never contradicts what you already sent. Auto-researches answers on support.brightcove.com and embeds them in the draft. Won't archive support emails if you're named, the customer is frustrated, or the thread is stalled. Nothing sends without your approval.
Inbox Zero
Also Built In
📊
Account Summary
Full 360° account view — Gmail, Brightcove Gateway, Granola, and Notion pulled in parallel. Surfaces cadence gaps, competitor risk, and renewal timing.
⚔️
Competitor Analysis
Structured battlecard from real context — Brightcove docs, email history, and past call notes cross-referenced for wins, losses, and objection responses.
🗂️
Notion Integration
Prepends structured follow-up content to each customer's Active Customers row in Notion — action items, resources, and one copy-paste email. No manual notes.
🧠
Always-On Research
Any unanswered question in a draft, prep card, or follow-up page triggers an auto-lookup on support.brightcove.com with a ✅ inline answer.

Brightcove Gateway
Salesforce data · Gong transcripts · Opportunities · Usage metrics · Contract details
Auto-Configured ✅

The Brightcove Gateway is the single connector that powers most of GOOSE's intelligence. It connects to Brightcove's internal BigQuery data warehouse and surfaces two major data sources — no separate Salesforce login or Gong API key required.

🏢 Salesforce Data
Account details · Active opportunities · Contract history · Usage entitlements · Quote data · Video Cloud event metrics
🎤 Gong Transcripts
Full call transcripts · Sales team call history · Searchable by account · Synced hourly from Salesforce
1
In Claude Desktop, click Customize in the left sidebar, then click Connectors, then click Connect your tools. Search for "Brightcove Gateway" and install the connector.
2
Authenticate with your Brightcove corporate credentials when prompted — this connects to the brightcove-lumenx-42 BigQuery project.
3
Once connected, restart Claude Desktop. The plugin will automatically use the Gateway for all account, Salesforce, and Gong queries.
✅ No IT ticket needed! One connector, two data sources. Authenticate with your existing Brightcove credentials — no Connected Apps, no Gong API keys, no admin involvement required.
ℹ️ The Gateway connects to BigQuery project brightcove-lumenx-42, dataset external_shared_views. Salesforce exports, contract data, entitlement usage, Video Cloud event metrics, and Gong transcripts — all in one place.

Installation

How to Get Started — 3 Steps

GOOSE is already installed on your Claude Desktop. Just connect your tools, type "start", and you're ready to go.

1
Connect your tools Open Claude Desktop. Click Customize in the left sidebar, then click Connectors, then click Connect your tools. A pop-up will appear — search for each tool and click Connect (~30 seconds each):
  • 📧 Gmail — connect it twice: once for reading, once for sending. Both use the same Brightcove Google account.
  • 📆 Google Calendar — read-only access to your calendar
  • 🗂️ Google Drive — read-only access for reference docs
  • 📓 Notion — sign in with your Brightcove Notion account
  • 🎙️ Granola (recommended) — if you use Granola for meeting notes
⚠️ If you skip this, Claude will still run — but email triage and daily call prep won't work until connectors are live.
⚡ Pro tip: Switch to Claude Sonnet — click the model name at the bottom of the chat window and select Claude Sonnet. It's 3× faster for these workflows with virtually no quality loss.
2
Open a new Cowork task and type "start" In Claude Desktop, go to the Cowork tab and click New Task. When the task opens, type "start" — GOOSE will detect it's your first time and launch the setup wizard automatically.

GOOSE will ask for:
  • Your name and role at Brightcove
  • How many customer calls you do per week
  • Your Brightcove email address
Then it connects to the shared Active Customers database in Notion and saves your profile. Total time: ~5 minutes.
💡 When Claude asks to take an action — click Always Allow. This lets workflows run without requiring your approval for every individual step.
3
You're all set! Once onboarding is complete, just type natural language in any Cowork task:
  • "Prep me for my calls today" → daily briefing
  • "Triage my inbox" → email triage
  • "Run call companion" → live call assistant
No slash commands to memorize — just talk to GOOSE like you'd talk to a colleague.

Reference

Available Commands

Type any of these in Claude Desktop after installing the plugin.

/prime
Run at the start of every session. Reads all context files and confirms Claude is up to speed.
/morning_schedule
Daily 7am briefing. Loads Google Calendar, classifies customer vs internal meetings, prints today's schedule.
/daily_prep
Generates a dark-theme HTML call prep page for today. Pulls calendar, enriches customer meetings with Gmail, Granola, Gong transcripts, and Salesforce account data via Brightcove Gateway.
/call_companion
Live call assistant. Monitors Granola notes, researches docs in real time, then creates a follow-up subpage under the customer's Active Customers row in Notion.
/email_triage
Scans inbox, categorizes by action, drafts responses, and archives noise. Always reviews with you before sending anything.
/call_prep [customer]
On-demand pre-call briefing for any account. Pulls Brightcove Gateway data, recent emails, Granola notes, and Notion history.
/account_summary [customer]
360° account view: 6 months of call history, full Brightcove Gateway pull, email threads, and health score.
/migrate-history
One-time migration. Moves your private Call Follow-Ups DB entries into the shared Active Customers database. Safe to re-run — duplicates are detected and skipped.
