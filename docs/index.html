Overview

What This Plugin Does

Brightcove Sales Co-Pilot plugs into Claude Desktop and gives you a set of commands that automate the most time-consuming parts of your sales workflow.

🌅
Daily Call Prep
Say "prep me for my calls today" and Claude pulls your full day together — Google Calendar, Granola meeting notes, and Gmail — then outputs a visual HTML briefing page. Any open customer question is auto-researched on support.brightcove.com and embedded with a ✅ before your first call.
Morning Ritual
📞
Live Call Companion
Monitors your Granola transcript in real time during a call. When it detects a trigger phrase, it deploys parallel agents to research answers from Brightcove docs and your account history — all while you keep talking. After the call: one Notion page + one copy-paste follow-up email, already written.
Real-Time
📬
Email Triage
Reads full email threads before drafting — never contradicts what you already sent. Auto-researches answers on support.brightcove.com and embeds them in the draft. Won't archive support emails if you're named, the customer is frustrated, or the thread is stalled. Nothing sends without your approval.
Inbox Zero
📊
Account Summary
Full 360° account view on demand — pulls Gmail threads, Brightcove Gateway data, Granola meeting notes, and Notion simultaneously. Surfaces cadence gaps, competitor risk, renewal timing, and upsell signals. One brief ready to share with your Account Executive or bring into an exec conversation.
Account Intel
⚔️
Competitor Analysis
Cross-references Brightcove support docs, your email history, and Granola call notes for how Brightcove has won (or lost) against a competitor before. Output: a structured battlecard with their strengths, your advantages, and objection responses — built from real context.
Competitive
🗂️
Notion Integration
After every call, automatically writes a structured Notion follow-up page: action items, resources, key intel, and one copy-paste follow-up email at the bottom covering all topics. Consistent format — [Customer] — [Date] — enriched with Granola and Brightcove Gateway data. No manual note-taking required.
Auto Follow-Up
🧠
Always-On Research Engine
Built into every command. Any unanswered technical question in a draft, prep card, triage summary, or follow-up page triggers an automatic lookup at support.brightcove.com. Answers appear inline with a ✅ Researched Answer marker. You never walk into a call with an open question still unanswered.
Auto-Research
Integrations

Platforms It Connects To

Most integrations are self-serve with no IT tickets required. Click any row for connection instructions.

🔵
Brightcove Gateway
Account data · Opportunities · Usage metrics · Contract details · Salesforce exports
Auto-Configured ✅

Used to pull account details, active opportunities, usage data, and contract information via Brightcove's internal BigQuery data warehouse. No OAuth or Salesforce Connected App required — connects via connector and uses your Brightcove credentials.

1
In Claude Desktop → Settings → Customize → Connectors, search for "Brightcove Gateway" and install the connector.
2
Authenticate with your Brightcove corporate credentials when prompted — this connects to the brightcove-lumenx-42 BigQuery project.
3
Once connected, restart Claude Desktop. The plugin will automatically use the Gateway for all account and usage queries.
✅ No IT ticket needed! The Brightcove Gateway connector is self-serve. Authenticate with your existing Brightcove credentials — no Connected Apps, no OAuth setup, no admin involvement required.
ℹ️ The Gateway connects to BigQuery project brightcove-lumenx-42, dataset external_shared_views. This includes enriched Salesforce exports, contract data, entitlement usage, and Video Cloud event metrics — all the data you'd otherwise need Salesforce access to pull manually.
🎤
Gong
Call recordings · Transcripts · Sales team call history
Auto-configured ✅

Gong call transcripts are automatically synced to Salesforce and accessible via the Brightcove Gateway — no separate API key required. Search transcripts, pull Sales team call history, and surface best practices directly from your Brightcove data warehouse.

🎟️ No setup needed: Gong transcripts are automatically available in your Brightcove Gateway instance. No API keys or IT tickets required.
📧
Gmail
Email read/search/draft · Inbox triage · Thread context
Sign in with Google

Used for reading email threads, drafting responses, and inbox triage. You'll sign in with your Brightcove Google account — no separate credentials needed.

1
In Claude Desktop → Settings → Customize → Connectors, search for "Gmail" and click Connect. Sign in with your Brightcove Google account.
2
Search for "Gmail" a second time and connect it again — one handles reading your inbox, one handles sending. Both use the same Google account.
3
Approve the Google permissions screen, then restart Claude Desktop.
ℹ️ The email triage command never auto-sends emails. It always shows you draft content and asks for confirmation before sending anything.
📆
Google Calendar
Schedule · Meeting classification · Daily timeline
Sign in with Google

Used in daily call prep to pull your schedule and classify meetings. Read-only — this plugin cannot create or modify calendar events.

1
In Claude Desktop → Settings → Customize → Connectors, search for "Google Calendar" and click Connect. Sign in with your Brightcove Google account.
2
Approve the permissions screen, then restart Claude Desktop.
✅ Read-only access only. This plugin cannot create, modify, or delete any calendar events.
🗂️
Google Drive
File access · Document context · Reference materials
Sign in with Google

Used for accessing reference documents and customer files. Sign in with your Brightcove Google account — same credentials as Gmail and Calendar.

1
In Claude Desktop → Settings → Customize → Connectors, search for "Google Drive" and click Connect. Sign in with your Brightcove Google account.
2
Approve the permissions screen, then restart Claude Desktop.
✅ Read-only access. Claude can search and read files you've shared, but cannot create, modify, or delete Google Drive files.
📓
Notion
Call follow-up pages · Account tracking · Knowledge base
Auto-Configured ✅

After each call, the plugin writes a structured follow-up page to Notion — action items, resources, and a copy-paste follow-up email. No manual database setup needed — onboarding creates your Call Follow-Ups and Customer Call Prep databases automatically.

1
Connect Notion in Claude Desktop → Settings → Customize → Connectors (sign in with your Brightcove Notion account)
2
That's it. During onboarding, Claude automatically creates your Call Follow-Ups and Customer Call Prep databases in Notion — no IDs to copy, no manual config.
✅ Zero manual setup! Notion databases are created automatically during first-run onboarding. You never need to touch database IDs, CLAUDE.md, or any config files.
🎙️
Granola
Meeting transcripts · Live call notes · AI summaries
Sign in with Granola

Used by Live Call Companion to monitor meeting notes in real time and pull transcripts post-call. Sign in with your corporate account — same as your other tools.

1
Install Granola on your Mac and sign in with your Brightcove account
2
In Claude Desktop → Settings → Customize → Connectors, search for "Granola" and click Connect
3
Sign in when prompted — Granola will automatically make your meeting transcripts accessible to Claude
✅ No IT ticket needed. Fully self-serve. Once connected, Granola automatically transcribes your meetings and Claude can reference them during and after calls.
Installation

How to Install — Step by Step

Takes about 10 minutes total. No terminal commands, no IT tickets, no technical knowledge needed.

🧾

Before You Start — Checklist

Have these ready before you begin. The install takes ~10 minutes but will stall if something is missing.

🖥️
Claude Desktop Required
Must be installed on your Mac. Download Claude Desktop →
📧
Google Account (Gmail + Calendar) Required
Have your Brightcove Google login ready — you'll connect Gmail and Google Calendar during install. No admin setup needed.
📓
Notion Account Required
You need a Notion account (free plan is fine). You don't need to set anything up — the plugin creates your databases automatically. Just make sure you can log in to Notion.
🔑
Brightcove Gateway Access Pre-Configured
Gives Claude access to account data, usage, and contract information. No separate credentials needed — just use your Brightcove Google login (same account you already connected above). No IT ticket or admin approval required.
🎙️
Granola Recommended
Provides real-time live transcription during customer calls. Without Granola, Call Companion will use your Gong call history for pre-call context (transcripts sync hourly) — but real-time monitoring requires Granola. Download Granola →
🔌
Connect your tools in Claude Desktop Do This First
Before downloading the plugin, connect your tools in Claude Desktop. Go to Settings → Customize → Connectors and connect each of the following (~30 seconds each):
  • 📧 Gmail — connect it twice: once for reading, once for sending. Both use the same Brightcove Google account.
  • 📆 Google Calendar — read-only access to your calendar
  • 🗂️ Google Drive — read-only access for reference docs
  • 📓 Notion — sign in with your Brightcove Notion account
  • 🎙️ Granola (recommended) — if you use Granola for meeting notes
1
Download the plugin file Click the ⬇ Download & Install Plugin button at the top of this page. Your browser will save a file called brightcove-sales-co-pilot.plugin to your Downloads folder.
2
Open the plugin in Claude Desktop Double-click the .plugin file in your Downloads folder. Claude Desktop will open automatically and show you a Save Plugin dialog.
💡 Double-click didn't open anything? Use the manual method instead:
  1. Open Claude Desktop manually
  2. Go to Cowork and click Start a new task
  3. Drag and drop the .plugin file from your Downloads folder into the Cowork window
  4. Type "install this plugin" and follow the prompts
3
Click Save in the dialog In the Save Plugin dialog, review the plugin name and description, then click Save. Claude Desktop will install the plugin files.
4
Choose your workspace folder A dialog will appear saying "Claude would like to Cowork in a folder." This is where the plugin will store your call prep files, follow-ups, and outputs — think of it as your Sales Co-Pilot filing cabinet.

Follow these steps exactly:
  1. Click Choose Folder in the dialog
  2. In the Finder window that opens, click Documents in the left sidebar
  3. Click the New Folder button (bottom-left of the Finder window)
  4. Name the folder Sales Co-Pilot and press Return
  5. Make sure Sales Co-Pilot is highlighted/selected, then click Open
5
Verify your connectors are active Go to Settings → Customize → Connectors and confirm Gmail (×2), Google Calendar, Google Drive, and Notion all show as connected. If any are missing, connect them now — it takes about 30 seconds each.
⚠️ If you skip this, Claude will still run — but email triage and daily call prep won't work until connectors are live.
6
Start a new Cowork session — onboarding starts automatically Open Claude Desktop, go to Cowork, and start a new task. Claude will immediately detect that it's your first time and begin the setup wizard — no commands to type.

Claude will ask for:
  • Your name and role at Brightcove
  • How many customer calls you do per week
  • Your Brightcove email address
Then it automatically creates your Notion workspace (Call Follow-Ups and Customer Call Prep databases) and saves everything. Total time: ~5 minutes.
💡 When Claude asks to take an action — click Always Allow. This lets workflows run without requiring your approval for every individual step.
🎉 Once onboarding is complete, just type natural language in Claude Desktop: "Prep me for my calls today", "Triage my inbox", or "Run call companion" — and Claude handles the rest.
Reference

Available Commands

Type any of these in Claude Desktop after installing the plugin.

/prime
Run at the start of every session. Reads all context files and confirms Claude is up to speed.
/morning_schedule
Daily 7am briefing. Loads Google Calendar, classifies customer vs internal meetings, prints today's schedule.
/daily_prep
Generates a dark-theme HTML call prep page for today. Pulls calendar, enriches customer meetings with Gmail and Granola context.
/call_companion
Live call assistant. Monitors Granola notes, researches docs in real time, then writes a Notion follow-up page after the call.
/email_triage
Scans inbox, categorizes by action, drafts responses, and archives noise. Always reviews with you before sending anything.
/call_prep [customer]
On-demand pre-call briefing for any account. Pulls Brightcove Gateway data, recent emails, Granola notes, and Notion history.
/account_summary [customer]
360° account view: 6 months of call history, full Brightcove Gateway pull, email threads, and health score.
